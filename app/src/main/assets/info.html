Thank you for choosing the Shopping List app!

The application allows you to easily add, change, mark an item as purchased and also remove it from the shopping list.

Brief instructions:

  • To add a new entry, click Add.
  • To change the description of the record or quantity, click on the required item and enter a new value.
  • To mark the purchased item, long-press the desired item until it turns dark.
  • To restore the purchased item, hold the required item again until it returns to its original color.
  • To delete an unnecessary item in the list, swipe it to the right or left.
  • To continue, click Accept.

Enjoy using the app.
